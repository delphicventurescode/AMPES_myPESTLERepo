# Probability Problems for "Data and Decisions"

## 1. Problem #1
A student picks one elective out of two categories:  
- **Humanities** (10 courses available)  
- **Management** (8 courses available)  

Three courses appear in *both* categories.

**Question:**  
If the student picks a course at random from the combined list, what is the probability that it is from Humanities AND Management?

---

## 2. Problem #2
In a survey of 300 MBA students:  
- 120 like *marketing*  
- 180 like *finance*  
- 70 like *both*

**Question:**  
If a student is chosen at random, what is the probability they like marketing **and** finance?

---

## 3. Problem #3
A quality-control inspector checks two independent machines:  
- Machine A produces a defective item with probability 0.04  
- Machine B produces a defective item with probability 0.06  

**Question:**  
What is the probability that both machines produce a defective item on the same day?

---
