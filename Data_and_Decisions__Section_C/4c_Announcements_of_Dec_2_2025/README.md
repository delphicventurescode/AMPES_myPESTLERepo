## 4. Problem #4
A website classifies visitors:  
- 40% arrive via mobile, 60% via desktop.  
- 25% of mobile visitors purchase something.  
- 10% of desktop visitors purchase something.

**Question:**  
What is the probability that a randomly chosen visitor purchased something **and** came from a mobile device?

## 5. Problem #5
A disease affects 2% of a population. A diagnostic test has:  
- **Sensitivity:** 95%  
- **Specificity:** 90%

A person tests positive.

**Question:**  
What is the probability they actually have the disease?

## 6. Problem #6
A company uses two suppliers for chips:  
- Supplier A: 70% of shipments, defect rate = 3%  
- Supplier B: 30% of shipments, defect rate = 7%

A randomly selected chip is defective.

**Question:**  
What is the probability that it came from Supplier B?
