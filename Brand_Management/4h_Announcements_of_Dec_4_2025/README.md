Qlip is an **AI-powered video-clipping platform** that automatically converts long-form videos (such as podcasts, interviews, and webinars) into **short, engaging, social-media-ready clips**.

* Adds **captions** and custom **branding elements**.
* **Reformats videos** for platforms like Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.

For brand managers, Qlip offers a fast, scalable way to:

* Repurpose existing content efficiently.
* Maintain consistent visual identity (branding).
* Increase audience reach without heavy manual editing.

As **short-form video** continues to dominate digital marketing, Qlip represents a valuable tool for enhancing **brand visibility** and **content efficiency**.
