Adobe Firefly is a **generative AI platform** created by Adobe to produce high-quality visual content ‚Äî including images, graphics, text effects, and design elements ‚Äî using simple natural-language prompts.

It is built directly into Adobe tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Express, allowing users to **instantly generate or modify visuals** within their existing workflows.

## üé® What It Does / Key Features

* **Text-to-image generation** for creating custom visuals on demand.
* **Generative fill & editing**, letting users add, remove, or transform objects in photos with realistic results.
* **AI-powered vector and design tools** for scalable branding assets.
* **Brand-safe content**, trained on Adobe-licensed and public-domain data, with options for IP indemnification (for enterprise users).
* **Style Kits/Custom Models:** Allows teams to train the AI on proprietary brand assets (logos, colors, style references) to ensure all generated content is highly consistent and on-brand.
